How to play

Guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries. A new word appears every day at midnight Eastern Time.

Type your guess and hit Enter. The row plays back in Morse, letter by letter, as it reveals the result.

  • Right letter, right spot
  • Right letter, wrong spot
  • Not in the word

Each guessed tile shows the Morse code for its letter — tap a tile to replay it. The chart (⋯) has the full alphabet.
